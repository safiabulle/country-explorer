About Country Explorer

Country Explorer is a simple website that helps users learn about countries around the world. Search any country and instantly view useful details such as its flag, capital city, region, and population.

Our Goal

To make country information easy to access in one place using a clean and user-friendly design.

Features

  • Search countries by name
  • View country flags
  • See capital cities
  • Check population details
  • Responsive on all devices

Why It Matters

Country Explorer makes learning about the world faster and easier by providing country information in one simple place.

Why Use It?

Country Explorer is perfect for students, travelers, and anyone curious about the world.
